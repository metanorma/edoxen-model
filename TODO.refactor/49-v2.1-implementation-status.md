  `Voting.on_motion`.
- `Decision#brought_by_motions_in(meeting:)` — derived from
  `meeting.motions` selecting those whose `resulting_decision` (or

  `meeting.components` finding the one whose `identifier` or `urn` ==

  `collection.decisions` selecting those whose `about_topics` includes
  `self.urn`. Storage SSOT: `Decision.about_topics`.
