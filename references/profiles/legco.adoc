Profile: legco (HK Legislative Council)
=======================================

Profile namespace: `legco`

Source schema: HK LegCo Open Data Hansard Database
https://www.legco.gov.hk/en/open-legco/open-data/hansard-database.html

OData service root: https://app.legco.gov.hk/OpenData/HansardDB/

The HK LegCo schema exposes 16 normalized entity types via OData.
Eight of these are domain-specific (not in Edoxen core) and live
as profile extensions under the `legco` namespace.


== Extension kinds


=== `bill`

A bill under consideration by the Council.

Hangs on: `Topic.documents[]` as a `TopicDocument` with `kind="bill"`,
plus this extension on the TopicDocument for LegCo-specific metadata.

Maps to: `Bills` + `BillStages` (LegCo OData)

Attributes:
- `billNumber` (String) — LegCo bill number (e.g., "2024年第87号")
- `stage` (String) — first_reading | second_reading | committee_stage | third_reading | resumption
- `introducedBy` (String) — Government | Member name
- `gazetteDate` (Date) — date the bill was gazetted
- `gazetteUrl` (String) — URL to gazette notice

Sample YAML:

[source,yaml]
----
documents:
  - identifier: hk-bill-2024-waste-disposal-amendment
    title: Waste Disposal (Amendment) Bill 2024
    kind: bill
    url: https://www.gld.gov.hk/egazette/english/gazette/toc.php
    extensions:
      - profile: legco
        kind: bill
        attributes:
          - key: billNumber
            value: "2024年第87号"
          - key: stage
            value: second_reading
          - key: introducedBy
            value: Government
----


=== `petition`

A petition presented by a Member.

Hangs on: `Topic.documents[]` as a `TopicDocument` with `kind="petition"`.

Maps to: `Petitions` (LegCo OData)

Attributes:
- `presentedBy` (String) — Member name(s)
- `signatories` (Integer) — number of signatures
- `datePresented` (Date)


=== `address`

A formal address by the Chief Executive, a Member, or a designated
public officer.

Hangs on: `Meeting.components[]` as a `MeetingComponent` with
`kind="address"`, plus this extension for LegCo-specific details.

Maps to: `Addresses` (LegCo OData)

Attributes:
- `speakerId` (String) — Speaker ID in LegCo's catalog
- `addressKind` (String) — CE_address | member_address | officer_address


=== `statement`

A formal statement by a designated public officer.

Hangs on: `Meeting.components[]` as a `MeetingComponent` with
`kind="statement"`.

Maps to: `Statements` (LegCo OData)

Attributes:
- `speakerId` (String)
- `subjectId` (String) — cross-reference to Subjects table


=== `question`

A question to the Government (oral, written, urgent, or supplementary).

Hangs on: `Topic.documents[]` as a `TopicDocument` with `kind="question"`.

Maps to: `Questions` (LegCo OData)

Attributes:
- `questionType` (String) — Oral | Written | Urgent | Supplementary
- `askedBy` (String) — Member name
- `answerUrl` (String) — URL to the Government's reply


=== `voting_result`

Voting result metadata (the per-vote member list lives in `Meeting.votings[].voteRecords`).

Hangs on: `Meeting.votings[]` as a `Voting` extension.

Maps to: `VotingResults` (LegCo OData)

Attributes:
- `sectionCode` (String) — LegCo section code (e.g., "b2d", "cwc")
- `bookmark` (String) — PDF bookmark name for deep-link (e.g., "EV_VOTED_00146")


=== `summoning_bell`

A quorum bell rung when fewer Members than the quorum are present.

Hangs on: `Meeting.extensions[]` directly on the Meeting.

Maps to: `SummoningBells` (LegCo OData)

Attributes:
- `sectionCode` (String)
- `timestamp` (DateTime) — when the bell was rung
- `bookmark` (String) — PDF deep-link


=== `subject`

A subject thread that can span multiple sittings via resumption.

Hangs on: `Topic.extensions[]` on a Topic.

Maps to: `Subjects` (LegCo OData, including `ResumptionOf`)

Attributes:
- `subjectId` (String) — LegCo's internal subject identifier
- `sectionCode` (String)
- `hansardType` (String) — English | Floor | Chinese
- `resumptionOf` (Date) — date of prior sitting where subject was first raised

Sample YAML:

[source,yaml]
----
topics:
  - identifier: legco-subject-waste-disposal-bill-2r
    title: Second Reading of Waste Disposal (Amendment) Bill 2024
    status: decided
    extensions:
      - profile: legco
        kind: subject
        attributes:
          - key: subjectId
            value: "12345"
          - key: sectionCode
            value: b2d
          - key: hansardType
            value: English
          - key: resumptionOf
            value: "2024-12-11"
----


== Profile declaration

Adopters using the LegCo profile should declare it once at the
top of their Meeting YAML:

[source,yaml]
----
extensions:
  - profile: legco
    kind: profile_declaration
    attributes:
      - key: source
        value: https://app.legco.gov.hk/OpenData/HansardDB/
      - key: term
        value: "8th Legislative Council"
      - key: termStartDate
        value: "2026-01-01"
----

This makes the profile's namespace explicit and links the
Meeting to its source dataset.
